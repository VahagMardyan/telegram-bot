Hi! This bot combines the convenience of *Telegram*, the power of *ChatGPT* and the fun of *Tinder*.

*The *Chatbot can:
1. Generate Tinder profile by description 😎
2. Write interesting and intriguing dating messages. 😘
3. Engage in correspondence on your behalf 😇🔥
4. You can practice your correspondence with the chatbot. 🥰


* Useful commands and links:* 
1. /start - main bot menu
2. /profile - generation of Tinder profile 😎
3. /opener - message for acquaintance 🥰
4. /message - correspondence on your behalf 😈
5. /date - correspondence with stars 🔥
6. /gpt - ask a question in GPT chat 🧠
